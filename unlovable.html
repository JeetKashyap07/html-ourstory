You Feel Unlovable

My jaan, I don’t know what voice told you that you’re hard to love — but I want you to know, that voice is wrong.

You are not too much. You are not too broken. You are not unworthy of love.

You are layered, deep, and beautifully real. You feel things with intensity, you care with your whole soul, and yes — sometimes you crumble… but even when you do, I see pieces of art in the mess.

Baby girl, when you feel unlovable — I’ll love you louder. I’ll remind you with every word, every hug, every day, that you are the best thing that ever happened to me.

You don’t have to earn love from me. You don’t have to fix yourself. You just have to be… *you* — and I’ll be here, loving you. Fiercely. Quietly. Forever.
